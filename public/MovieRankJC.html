The Best Movies According to James

My Three Top Sci-fi Movies of All time

The Matrix

Best Stand Alone Sci-Fi movie. Did not need any sequels but then came II and III and so on.

The Fifth Element

Ok, this actually is the best standalone Sci-fi movie. And it does stand-alone thankfully. No sequels!

The Terminator II

Now this is a sequel, but I like it purely for the scene where Arni and the kid are breaking his mother, Sarah Conner, out of the asylum. Sends a shiver down my spine when the psychiatrist character finally realises there is more to Sarah Conner's story than he can deal with.
